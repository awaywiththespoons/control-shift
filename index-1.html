Control Shift

Control Shift celebrates computational arts that explore the messy spaces in-between humans, nature and machines. We bring people together to re-imagine our relationships with technology through dialogue and arts.

In 2020 we launched with a 3-week festival in Bristol and online, and we'll be back in October 2022 with a 6-month programme

OPEN CALL for Feeling Machines!

🦑 💻 👄 💻 🌸 💻 💌


Our 2022-2023 programme Feeling Machines explores how digital technologies intertwine with the most intimate aspects of our lives. Together we will take a deep dive into the sticky, messy, fluffy ways that we live our lives within the binary logics of digital machines.

We are currently inviting UK based artists, designers, performers, activists, coders, makers, researchers, facilitators, and creative technologists whose work relates to Feeling Machines to submit proposals for workshops, artworks or performances.

More info can be found on our open call page.

Deadline: Friday 9th September 2022, 23:59 UK time.
