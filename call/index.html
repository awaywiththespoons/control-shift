    Control Shift Open Call is now closed


    Propose an artwork, workshop or provocation
    Work with us. Join the Control Shift Network

    Control Shift is an exciting new arts programme coming to Bristol (UK) in June 2020 exploring creative and critical approaches to technology, rooted in embodiment and materiality.

    We are looking for artworks, workshops, and provocations.

    We can offer varying fees and materials budgets available depending on the scale and form of work:

    • Artwork presentation fee of £500 plus negotiable production budget.
    • Workshop fee of £220 plus materials.
    • Speaker fee of up to £120.
    • Plus additional travel, access, and expenses bursaries.

    How To Apply
