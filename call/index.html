We are inviting artists, educators and creative folk to work with us for our 2022/2023 programme 'Feeling Machines'... The call is open to UK based artists and we are specifically looking for artists based in the South West.

Theme

This year’s Control Shift theme is Feeling Machines, where we are exploring how digital technologies intertwine with the most intimate aspects of our lives. The programme takes a deep dive into the sticky, messy, fluffy ways that we live our lives within the binary logics of digital machines.

In a time when phone apps define who our friends and lovers are, when AI have become artists, and algorithms decide the music that moves us we ask what it feels like to feel with machines. How often do we stop and question our relationships with machines? How much are we aware of the technologies sensing our bodies? What kind of emotions do scrolling through our social media feeds evoke? Are we craving touch outside our online grids or longing for our phones to fit differently against our skin?
