Control Shift Open Call 2022


Propose an artwork or workshop
Work with us.

Control Shift is an exciting new arts programme based in Bristol (UK) exploring creative and critical approaches to technology.

We are looking for artworks and workshops for our 2022/2023 programme 'Feeling Machines'.

We can offer varying fees and materials budgets available depending on the scale and form of work:

  • Artwork presentation fee of £500 plus negotiable production budget.
  • Workshop fee of £220 plus materials.
  • Speaker fee of up to £120.
  • Plus additional travel, access, and expenses bursaries.

How To Apply

To apply please download and read the full "Call Out" information.
Use the "How To Apply" document and send to Martha King martha.kng@gmail.com
